This Addendum forms part of the agreement between the customer (controller) and Mulholland, Inc. (processor) for the processing of personal data in Marzy.

1. Roles

The customer is the controller and Mulholland is the processor of customer personal data. Mulholland processes such data only on documented instructions from the customer.

2. Scope of processing

  • Subject matter — provision of the Marzy service.
  • Duration — for the term of the agreement.
  • Nature and purpose — running the back-office workflows the customer configures.
  • Data subjects — the customer's employees, clients, and contacts.

3. Confidentiality

Personnel authorized to process personal data are bound by confidentiality obligations.

4. Security

Mulholland maintains technical and organizational measures appropriate to the risk, including encryption in transit and at rest, scoped access, isolated workspaces, and an immutable audit trail. Details are described on our security page.

5. Sub-processors

The customer authorizes Mulholland to engage the sub-processors listed on our sub-processors page. We provide notice of changes and an opportunity to object.

6. Data subject requests

Mulholland assists the customer in responding to data subject requests, taking into account the nature of the processing.

7. Personal data breaches

Mulholland notifies the customer without undue delay after becoming aware of a personal data breach affecting customer data.

8. International transfers

Where personal data is transferred across borders, the parties rely on appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses.

9. Deletion and return

On termination, Mulholland deletes or returns customer personal data at the customer's choice, except where retention is required by law. A certificate of destruction is available on request.

10. Audits

Mulholland makes available information necessary to demonstrate compliance and allows for audits in line with the agreement.
