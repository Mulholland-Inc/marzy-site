This policy explains what data Mulholland, Inc. ("Mulholland," "we") collects when you use Marzy, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Who we are

Mulholland is the company behind Marzy, an AI agent for the back office. For customer workspaces, our customers are the controllers of the data they bring to Marzy, and we act as a processor on their behalf. This policy covers data for which we are the controller — primarily website visitors and account administrators.

Information we collect

  • Account information — name, work email, company, and role.
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, and diagnostic logs.
  • Connected systems — metadata about the systems you connect, under scoped access.
  • Communications — messages you send us and demo requests.

How we use information

We use information to provide and improve Marzy, including to:

  • Operate your workspace and run the workflows you configure.
  • Secure the service and detect abuse.
  • Communicate with you about your account and service changes.
  • Meet legal and compliance obligations.

We do not sell personal data, and we never train shared models on customer data.

Legal bases

Where required, we rely on one or more of the following: performance of a contract, your consent, our legitimate interests in operating the service, and compliance with legal obligations.

Sharing

We share data with vetted sub-processors that help us run the service, under contracts that require appropriate safeguards. A current list is maintained on our sub-processors page.

Retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purposes above, then delete or anonymize it. Customer workspace data is retained per our customer agreement.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise them, contact privacy@mulholland.inc.

International transfers

Where data is transferred across borders, we use appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses.

Changes

We'll update this policy as the service evolves and note the effective date above. Material changes will be communicated to account administrators.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email privacy@mulholland.inc.
