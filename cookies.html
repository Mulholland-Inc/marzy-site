This policy explains how Marzy uses cookies and similar technologies, and how you can control them.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use them and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and understand how the site is used.

Categories we use

  • Strictly necessary — sign-in, security, and core functionality. These can't be turned off.
  • Preferences — remember settings like your selected workspace.
  • Analytics — aggregate, privacy-respecting usage measurement to improve the product.

We do not use cookies for cross-site advertising.

Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings and through any consent banner we present. Blocking strictly necessary cookies may break parts of the service.

Third parties

Some cookies are set by providers we use for analytics and fonts. These providers are listed on our sub-processors page.

Changes

We'll update this policy as our use of cookies changes and note the effective date above.
