How we protect your data

Scoped, revocable access

Every connection is OAuth-scoped to exactly what a workflow needs, and revocable in one click.

Encrypted end to end

Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Secrets live in a managed vault, never in logs.

Isolated workspaces

Each workspace runs in its own scoped environment. Your data is never co-mingled.

Immutable audit trail

Every action keeps its source, reasoning, and exact change — and can't be altered after the fact.

Human in the loop

Nothing runs unattended unless you trust it. Everything else waits for approval.

SSO & SCIM

Enterprise identity, provisioning, and role-based access out of the box.
