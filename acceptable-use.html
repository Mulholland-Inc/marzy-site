This policy describes activities that are not allowed on Marzy. It's incorporated into our Terms of Service.

You may not

  • Use Marzy to violate any law or regulation.
  • Connect systems or upload data you don't have the right to use.
  • Attempt to access another customer's workspace or data.
  • Probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of the service without authorization.
  • Interfere with or disrupt the integrity or performance of the service.
  • Use the service to send spam or unlawful communications.
  • Reverse engineer the service except as permitted by law.
  • Use the service to build a competing product by copying its design or behavior.

Automated actions

You're responsible for the workflows you configure and the actions you authorize Marzy to take. Keep approval thresholds appropriate to the risk of each workflow.

Reporting abuse

To report a violation or suspected abuse, email abuse@mulholland.inc. We investigate all reports.

Enforcement

We may suspend or terminate access for violations of this policy, and will cooperate with law enforcement where required.

Contact

Questions about acceptable use? Email legal@mulholland.inc.
